"I guess", "It seems that".

There are many forms for this one.
### See Also

- [[-려나 보다]]
- [[-나 보다]]
- [[-가 보다]]

### Examples

- 내가 오늘도 숙제를 못하려나 봐.
    "I guess I might not do homework today too."

많이 바쁜가 봐요.
    "I guess they are very busy."

자는가 봐요.
    "I guess they are sleeping."
